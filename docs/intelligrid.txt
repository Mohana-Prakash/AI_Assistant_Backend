IntelliGrid is a carbon credit offset platform.  
It allows users to buy, sell, and track carbon credits.  
Superadmin can select multiple credits and sell them to consumers.  
Consumers can view their purchased credits, transaction history, and offset impact.  
Additionally, users can earn ranks once they reach specific carbon offset thresholds:  
- 2000 tCO₂e – Platinum  
- 700 tCO₂e – Gold  
- 150 tCO₂e – Silver
